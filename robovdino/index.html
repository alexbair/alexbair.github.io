Description

Throw Garbage At Dinosaurs! RoboVDino is 1-5 player cooperative carnage!

History

RoboVDino started life in 2015 as an HTML5 top-down shooter for Marshall Public Library's Little Tiny Con in the Middle of Nowhere in Pocatello, Idaho. Starting in 2017, Alex picked up RoboVDino again and spent 14 months rewriting it in Unity, improving the visuals, collecting feedback from play-tests, and redesigning RoboVDino from a one-player arcade shooter into a cooperative collecting adventure.

Features

  • Local cooperative action with up to 5 players
  • Gamepad support for comfortable party play
  • Throw garbage at dinosuars to knock them out
  • Pick up KO Dinos and throw them into the goal before time runs out
  • Very normal dinosaur habitats like the Dino-shore, RAWRcade, and Stock Rex-change
  • Fire breathing t-rexs, charging dimetrodons, and swinging stegos hunt you down... if you don't catch them first
  • More than a dozen things to throw at dinos like sandwiches, hockey pucks, rockets, a duck, nachos, and even other dinosaurs
